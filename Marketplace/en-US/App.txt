'nanapho news' is a news reader application for 'nanapho' that is Windows Phone news website.

Enabling push notification, you can receive nanapho's updates. Pin this app to your start screen to receive tile notification.

*SOME CONTENTS SUCH AS BLOG TITLES AND BLOG ARTICLES ARE DISPLAYED IN JAPANESE.
*PUSH NOTIFICATION FUNCTIONALITY IS BETA VERSION. IT MAY NOT WORK CORRECTLY.

Versions
1.1.0.0: Improved push notification functionality. Article is opened in an internal browser.
1.0.0.0: First release.
